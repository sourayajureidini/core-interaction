I was born in Paris in 1995 and raised in Beirut, Lebanon. My background and upbringing has had an indelible influence
on my work and perspective as an artist. This kaleidoscopic childhood made me sensitive to the arts as I now work as a graphic designer and a street life photographer on the side.  

I find beauty in so many different things: objects, buildings, humans, anything at all. Living in a country with that much history and authenticity influenced the love i feel towards history itself.

 I appreciate anything that is vintage which
is consciously and unconsciously reflected it in my work aesthetics. I often use modern layouts and bright colors. I consider my designs minimalistic. 

My photography is colorful and symmetrical which comes from my appreciation for architecture and geometrical shapes.
I embrace photographing things that seemed basic, ordinary and which are largely taken for granted.
